Start Page • Quick Help

  • Open Admin: ⚙️, Alt+A, or triple‑click top‑left corner.
  • Admin layout: Tabs + collapsible cards per section (modules can inject sections).
  • Favorites: Groups; drag tiles to reorder/move across groups.
  • Weather: Pill opens details (module). Admin → Settings shows Weather section.
  • Quickbar: YouTube, Amazon, Google, MSN, Spotify (right‑click Spotify for quick actions).
  • Backup: Exports **core + modules**; restores and skips missing modules (keeps orphaned state).
